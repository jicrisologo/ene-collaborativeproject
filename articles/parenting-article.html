Our Tips for Good Parenting

Written by: Ross Batacan, Joaquin Crisologo, Jayson Lim



Welcome to our webpage dedicated to providing valuable tips for good parenting, specifically focused on navigating the challenges of helicopter parenting. We understand that parenting is a complex journey, and our goal is to empower you with practical guidance to strike a healthy balance between protective instincts and allowing your child to flourish as an independent individual.

In her article, Blood outlines a few of the most important reminders to ensuring that your child grows up in a safe and comfortable environment without encroaching on their personal freedoms.


- Allow your child the freedom to make decisions, take risks, and learn from their experiences. Offer guidance, but also trust in their ability to overcome obstacles.
- Foster open communication by making your child feel at ease to communicate their feelings, opinions, and worries. Talk to them in depth and actively listen to comprehend their viewpoint.
- Set clear, acceptable boundaries that provide a balance between safety and freedom. Give direction while giving room for development and self-expression.
