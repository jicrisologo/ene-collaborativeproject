Our Playlist

Written by: Ross Batacan, Joaquin Crisologo, Jayson Lim



Welcome to our music playlist dedicated to the topic of helicopter parenting! In this curated collection of songs, we explore the themes and emotions surrounding this parenting style. Through the power of music, we aim to delve deeper into the complexities, offering a thought-provoking and immersive experience.

Helicopter parenting, with its well-intentioned but often suffocating tendencies, has become a topic of significant issue in today's society. As children navigate their path towards independence and self-discovery, the boundaries set by overprotective parents can spark a longing for freedom and autonomy. Our playlist shines a spotlight on the emotional journey of children who find themselves yearning to break free from the shackles of excessive control.

So, sit back, tune in, and let the music guide you.
