Under the Hover - A Documentary

“Under the Hover” delves into the intimate lives of families entangled in the complex web of helicopter parenting. Through these raw and emotional interviews, we witness the struggles of children longing for freedom and autonomy, and parents finding the balance between safeguarding their loved ones and allowing them to grow. This documentary sheds light on the consequences of helicopter parenting and explores the profound impact it has on family dynamics.
